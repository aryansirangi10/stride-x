Frequently Asked Questions

Each custom order is hand-dyed by artisans in our bespoke laboratory. A flat +₹1,500 premium is added to the base silhouette price to cover specialized compound dyes, nitrogen finishing treatments, and custom inspection.

Bespoke models require 3-5 business days for fabrication, curing, and drying. Once finalized, they are shipped via insured priority air delivery, arriving in 1-2 days. You can track this live in your Order History.

Because custom silhouettes are individually crafted to your dye specifications and sizing choice, we do not accept returns. However, we do offer a Fit Guarantee—if they do not fit, we will alter them free of charge.
